About

The Actor

A lot of people told Chase Monigle he should be an actor — and he took that seriously. Born and raised in Indialantic, Florida and a graduate of the Florida Institute of Technology, Chase brings a natural charisma and effortless screen presence to everything he steps into.

Charismatic, high-energy, and genuinely social, Chase connects with people instantly — on camera and off. He's athletic, driven, and the kind of person who lights up a room without trying. Whether it's a commercial, an on-camera interview, or a dramatic scene, he shows up ready and fully committed.

Chase is completely open to any role and actively seeking new opportunities. He's available for auditions, interviews, and on-set work across Florida and beyond.
